Our Mission

Integrated solutions that build East Africa from within.

Edgar Global Ventures Ltd was founded on a single conviction: that East Africa's sustainable growth requires solutions that work across technology, agriculture, trade and infrastructure — together, not in isolation.

From our Kampala headquarters, we serve clients across Uganda, Kenya, Tanzania, Rwanda and South Sudan — helping farmers increase yields, businesses scale digitally, exporters reach global markets, and communities access world-class infrastructure.

Our Journey

15 years of
building East Africa.

2010
EGV Founded in Kampala
Edgar Global Ventures Ltd incorporated with a focus on cross-border trade facilitation across East Africa.
2013
Technology Division Launched
EGV Technology created to serve the growing demand for software and IT infrastructure in Uganda and the region.
2016
Agriculture Division Established
Launched farmer training programmes and agri-input supply services across Northern and Central Uganda.
2020
Infrastructure Division Added
Began road construction, water utilities and commercial building projects in response to growing government contracts.
2024
Online Agriculture Shop Live
Launched the EGV e-commerce shop with 26 products, MTN/Airtel Mobile Money payment and nationwide Uganda delivery.
2026
Expanding Across East Africa
Active operations in Uganda, Kenya, Tanzania, Rwanda and South Sudan with 500+ trade partners across the region.
