About EGV

A diversified multi-sector company delivering technology, agriculture, trade and infrastructure across East Africa since 2010.

Our Mission

Built from the ground up for East Africa.

Edgar Global Ventures Ltd was founded with a clear conviction: that East Africa's growth requires integrated solutions across technology, agriculture, trade and infrastructure — not in silos.

From Kampala we serve clients across Uganda, Kenya, Tanzania and Rwanda — helping farmers boost yields, businesses scale digitally, exporters reach new markets, and communities access reliable infrastructure.
