Privacy Policy

How Edgar Global Ventures Ltd collects, uses and protects your personal information.

Last updated: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use our website or agriculture shop, we may collect your name, email, phone number, delivery address and payment details. We also collect browsing data such as IP addresses and cookies to improve your experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to process orders, arrange delivery, communicate about purchases, and improve our services. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Security

We use HTTPS encryption and industry-standard protocols to protect your data. Our systems are regularly reviewed for security vulnerabilities.

4. Cookies

We use essential cookies for session management and cart functionality. Optional analytics cookies can be declined using the banner shown when you first visit the site.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. Contact us at privacy@egv.co.ug or call +256 700 000 000.
