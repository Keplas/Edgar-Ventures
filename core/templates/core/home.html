Our Story

Built from the ground up
for East Africa.

Edgar Global Ventures Ltd was founded with a clear conviction: that East Africa's growth requires integrated solutions across technology, agriculture, trade and infrastructure working together — not in silos. Fifteen years on, that conviction drives everything we do.

From Kampala we serve clients across Uganda, Kenya, Tanzania and Rwanda — helping farmers boost yields, businesses scale digitally, exporters reach new markets, and communities access reliable infrastructure.
